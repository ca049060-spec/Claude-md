The job
The one thing it needs

Supercharger rebuild

100-HOUR SERVICE · DUE NOW
Inside the supercharger — the kit renews everything highlighted.

Why it's not optional: as the clutch washers wear, the clutch slips. Left too long it can shed metal into the engine — turning a ~$400 job into a new motor. The plastic bearing cages also go brittle with heat over time (that's the "2-year" half of the rule).
