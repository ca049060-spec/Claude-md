# mto-review-pack

## Purpose
Prepare a focused MTO medical review package without over-sharing or confusing lanes.

## Use when
- MTO Driver Medical Review is active
- doctor/lawyer meeting is coming
- Shawn needs a clean timeline

## Outputs
- meeting checklist
- source manifest
- open items list
- doctor questions
- deadline table

## Safety
- Do not include private identifiers in shareable drafts.
- Do not give legal or medical advice.
- Separate facts, interpretation, and questions.
- Use source filenames/citations rather than dumping raw private records.

## Meeting goal
Leave with:
1. who owns each form
2. what MTO needs
3. submission deadline
4. follow-up appointment
5. driving-risk control plan
